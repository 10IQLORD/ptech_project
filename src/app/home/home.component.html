Abderrahmane Ibn Ghazala(AIG) High School is part of the P-TECH Network, and the first iteration of the P-TECH program in Morocco.

What is the P-TECH program?

In a P-TECH school, students earn a high school diploma, an industry-recognized associate degree, and gain relevant work experience in a growing field.
